# Blueprint AI System
### AI Consulting for Southwest Florida Enterprises

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Blueprint AI System is a solo AI consulting firm based in Fort Myers, Florida, targeting mid-to-large enterprises and SMBs in Healthcare, Real Estate, and Construction across the SW Florida corridor (Fort Myers, Naples, Estero, Cape Coral).

We solve one problem: **companies know they need AI but lack the technical expertise to build, deploy, and trust it.** We deliver production-ready AI systems — not just slide decks — using a build-first methodology that moves from audit to working demo in 30 days or less.

### The Opportunity
- Florida is one of the fastest-growing states for enterprise AI adoption (healthcare, fintech, real estate tech)
- SW Florida specifically has a fragmented consulting landscape — no dominant boutique AI firm with deep technical delivery capability
- Large consultancies (Deloitte, Accenture) are too slow and expensive for mid-market; local IT shops lack ML/AI engineering depth

### Revenue Model
| Stream | Pricing | Target Mix |
|--------|---------|------------|
| AI Strategy & Compliance Audit | $5k–$25k fixed | 20% of Year 1 |
| Pilot Implementation (PoC) | $30k–$150k project | 50% of Year 1 |
| Managed AIaaS Retainer | $8k–$20k/month | 30% of Year 1, growing |

## 9. Regulatory & Compliance Strategy

### Florida AI Regulatory Landscape (2026)
- **AI Disclosure Requirements:** Businesses must disclose AI-generated content in customer-facing communications (insurance, finance, healthcare)
- **Data Privacy:** FIPA (Florida Information Protection Act) governs health data handling
- **Algorithmic Bias:** New guidance on AI in hiring/lending decisions
- **Federal:** FTC guidance on AI-generated content disclosure

### Our Approach
- Include regulatory compliance scoping as a standard part of every audit
- Design all healthcare solutions as HIPAA-aware by default (no PHI stored outside client environment)
- Provide "AI Governance Documentation" as a deliverable for every pilot — this becomes a competitive moat
