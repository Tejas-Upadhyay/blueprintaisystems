## General Tips for SW Florida Outreach
1. **Mention your location** — "based in Fort Myers" builds trust vs. generic remote consultants
2. **Lead with the demo** — "I have a working demo" is far more compelling than "I offer consulting services"
3. **Keep it short** — 3–4 sentences max, with a clear low-friction ask
4. **Follow up 5 days later** — "Just following up on my note about the AI demo. No pressure if timing isn't right — happy to circle back when it makes sense."
